As a common Latin aphorism reads, "knowledge is power." These detailed materials, created when the ideas were most fresh after the conclusion of my senior year, have a singular purpose: to help future New Trier students attain success.

While these documents will not do your work for you, they will give you often unsaid requisite information that will help you orient yourself in your environment. After all, you are at New Trier. More than you know is within your grasp.
