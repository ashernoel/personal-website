Deep Learning in Natural Language Processing

Class Information:

The goal of this class is to provide an introduction to deep learning and natural language processing. As a result of completing the course, students will gain exposure to the field, the course's resources, and a completed project.

The standard class will consist of a 30 minute lecture and 30 minute workshop. After class, enrichment problems can be submitted for brownie points!

Course materials will be uploaded here after each class.
